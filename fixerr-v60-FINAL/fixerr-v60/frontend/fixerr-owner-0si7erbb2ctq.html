📞 What to do when a booking comes in
1. Check Requests tab
Every new booking appears in Requests. You see customer name, phone, service, address, and estimate.
2. Call the customer
Their phone number is in the request. Call within 2 hours to confirm details and assign a professional.
3. Update status
Change request status to "Confirmed" once you've spoken to the customer and assigned a pro.
4. Mark Complete
After the job is done, mark as "Completed". Your 15% commission is tracked automatically.
