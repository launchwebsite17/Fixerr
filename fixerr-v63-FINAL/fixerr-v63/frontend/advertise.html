Common questions

How is pricing determined?
Pricing depends on placement type, target cities, campaign duration, and exclusivity. Since we're in soft launch, early advertisers get introductory rates — submit an inquiry below to get a custom quote.
Can I target only India or only the US?
Yes. You can run a campaign in just Indian cities, just US cities, or both — and narrow further to specific cities within either market.
What creative formats do you support?
A logo or icon, a short tagline, and a call-to-action button linking to your site. Simple by design — it keeps the customer experience clean while still surfacing your brand.
How do I track performance?
Every ad tracks impressions (times shown) and clicks. We'll share a performance summary with you regularly, and a self-serve dashboard is planned for a future release.
Is there a minimum commitment?
No long-term contracts during our launch phase. Campaigns can run on a monthly basis so you can test results before committing further.
